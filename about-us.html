About Us

Who We Are

FlashDrop Pro was created to solve a simple but overlooked problem: Most file-sharing tools don’t need to see your data — yet they do.

Cloud uploads, account systems, and tracking have become the default. We believe they shouldn’t be.

Our Philosophy

We follow one core principle: If we don’t need your data, we should never touch it.

  • We do not store your files or keep logs.
  • We do not build user profiles or monetize data.

How It Works

Using WebRTC technology, files are sent directly between users. Our servers are used only for minimal signaling.

Who It Is For

Individuals who value privacy, developers, remote teams, and journalists.

ミッション

FlashDrop Proは、多くのファイル共有ツールが本来必要のないユーザーデータにアクセスしているという問題を解決するために作成されました。
